373 Bubble Tea Shop

When someone says Bubble tea, they are referencing a traditionally cold-served beverage that has a tea base, a milk or fruit flavor, and edible pearlsinside a cup. Bubble tea has many aliases which tends to confuse people. You may have heard one or many of these phrases used, but you should know they all mean the same thing: bubble tea, pearl tea, bubble milk tea, boba, boba fruit tea or tapioca tea. Bubble Tea has become increasingly popular over the past few years. This is in large part due to the new and unique consumption experience of having tasty edible pearls in a beverage!

373 Bubble Tea Shop is owned and operated by Taiwanese immigrants. We have been making and selling Bubble tea (or) Boba Tea for decades. We would love for you to visit our store in Chinatown, Boston and have one of the most blissful taste experiences by having our authentic bubble tea!!
